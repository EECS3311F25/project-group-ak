**Release Goal** 

Deliver secure cross-platform mobile app where a group of users can collaboratively plan, manage, and keep track of their trip itineraries with AI-powered features. It can present the itineraries through a user-friendly and interactive map, calendar, or overview interface.

**Scope of the project (in terms of Epics/Key Features)**

1. Account Services (including Authentication)  
2. Home View  
3. Trip View  
4. Calendar View  
5. Map View  
6. AI Features
